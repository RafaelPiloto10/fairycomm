What is fairycomm?

fairycomm is a Chrome extension that automatically finds and suggests items you want from local businesses.

Our purpose.

To support local businesses in competing in the online market against corporations such as Amazon.

How it works.

Local businesses register themselves and their items on our website. Users with the chrome extenstion installed will receive suggestions for the items they are searching for while browsing.
